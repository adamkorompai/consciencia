My Journey

Awakening to Wholeness

For thirty years, I worked at the heart of global transformation, navigating the corridors of power, advocating for climate action, nature restoration and healthy food systems, and founding Conscience, Brussels' first sustainability agency to champion ambitious protection of Mother Earth.

Through this work, I discovered a profound truth: We cannot create the world we long for whilst running on empty. Lasting change requires us to lead from wholeness, not depletion.

It became clear the leaders and changemakers I was advising needed more than strategy: they needed to move towards vitality, build nervous system resilience and lead from their full capacity.

Awakening

My awakening to myself began with my children. Whilst the birth of my twin sons showed me that their future depended on a better world, inspiring my career shift to sustainability, it was the birth of my daughter that awoke me to the deep injustice of the Divine Feminine. This moved me to campaign also for social justice and, ultimately, to reclaim the sacred energy of the Divine Feminine within myself, shifting my focus from outer protest to inner power.

Like many women, after decades of taking care of everyone else, I found myself outgrowing relationships that no longer nourished me. I needed to remember who I was beneath all the doing.

The Journey

This longing to nurture my own soul led me on a decade-long journey, travelling from the corridors of power to the sacred sites of India, Peru, Mexico, Egypt, China and Japan. I studied ancient wisdom and somatic practices, discovering how to bridge the power of the intellect with the intelligence of the heart.

These two worlds, once separate, have now converged into a single path. I now understand that global transformation and personal transformation are inseparable. Without inner peace, how can we attain the conditions for world peace.

CONSCIENCIA

My offering, CONSCIENCIA, is the culmination of this integration. Today I help transition leaders step into their fullest vitality and power through embodied practices that release what no longer serves, recalibrate the nervous system and reconnect them to their deepest wisdom, so they can step into their authentic power, calm, clear and courageous.

Why I Wrote This Book

Business as usual is no longer possible. Global heating is worsening, oceans are warming and peace is fragile. We are in a state of collective distress as instability, insecurity and inertia envelop us.

What if the future were feminine?

I began my interviews in the summer of 2024, in the aftermath of political pushback on climate action and nature protection.

I observed the power and grace of women changemakers, their courage and determination, their optimism and raw hope. I noted their ways of working and their stamina, sustaining them like an invisible inner force.

I was called to celebrate and uplift these women changemakers, often working for decades resolutely, quietly, with creativity and determination. In doing so I discovered their shared superpowers. Their collective power came from the breath, from their energy centres, their life force within.

Once the chakras are aligned, everything is possible, the wheel starts to turn and unstoppable momentum is unleashed.

The 50 women I interviewed, and millions more like them, are powerful, and together they are unstoppable.

This book is in gratitude to the women who trusted me to tell their stories, it is their book. To the women who uplift me every day and the men who are already committed to a feminine future. To my beloved children Luc, Jasper and Penelope, you deserve a better future.
